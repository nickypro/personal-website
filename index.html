Hi I'm Nicky Pochinkov.

I'm a big Science nerd currently studying Theoretical Physics at Trinity College Dublin after getting a perfect score in my Leaving Cert. I'm a fan of all thing STEM and love learning new things and facing difficult problems. I have represented Ireland in both the International Maths Olympiad, and the International Chemistry Olympiad. I also achieved an Honourable Mention in the Benelux Maths Olympiad, and got through to the national finals of the Irish Programming and Linguistics Olympiads.

To enjoy myself, I like to play games casually. I enjoy games such as Terraria, Minecraft, Civilisation V, games from the Anno series, and any other games my friends might be playing. I also like to spend time with people I know by doing things such as getting food and chatting with them in the evenings, or going on long walks with them, or playing board games and role-playing games.
